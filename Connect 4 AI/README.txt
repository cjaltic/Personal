Connect 4 AI

Python implementation of Connect 4 and a competitive AI player.  AI uses a min-max tree
and alpha-beta pruning to choose the best next move.  Heuristic function is used to score
the boards, heuristic function considers how many n-in a row streaks the player already
has as well as how many n-in a row streaks are incapable of becoming 4-in a row streaks.
